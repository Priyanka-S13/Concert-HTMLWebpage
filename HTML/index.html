COIMBATORE

Vijay Antony is set to perform live in Coimbatore at the CODISSIA Ground on June 8, 2024, as part of his South Tour. This concert will be a musical extravaganza featuring a mix of acoustic, folk, hip hop, instrumental, and rap genres, all performed in Tamil. The event is designed to be a family-friendly experience suitable for all age groups, lasting three hours.

Tickets for the concert are available in several categories, ranging from Rs. 750 for Silver seating to Rs. 29,999 for the Stage category. Attendees can expect an electrifying performance from Vijay Antony, who is renowned for his hit compositions and dynamic stage presence.

Concert Highlights

  • Live performance by Vijay Antony
  • Diverse music genres: acoustic, folk, hip hop, instrumental, rap
  • Family-friendly event
  • Special stage effects and visual displays

FAQs

What time does the concert start?

The concert starts at 7:00 PM.

Can I buy tickets at the venue?

Tickets are recommended to be purchased online in advance, but a limited number may be available at the venue.

Is there an age restriction for the concert?

The concert is suitable for all age groups. However, children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult.
